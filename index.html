Our Story

Built on Trust.
Driven by Value.

What began as Uhuru Discount Centre in 1991 — founded by the Mogaka family with a bold vision for accessible wholesale retail — has grown into South Western Kenya's most trusted wholesale and retail destination.

🏆
Market Leader Since 1991
Ouru has long held a commanding position in wholesale business across South Western Kenya — a legacy built on fair prices and unmatched product variety.
📍
7 Counties. One Standard.
With branches in Kisii, Homabay, Migori, Nyamira, Bomet, Kericho and Transmara — Ouru brings wholesale prices closer to every retailer and family in the region.
🤝
1,000+ Products. One Destination.
From the smallest retailer restocking shelves to the largest contractor sourcing materials — Ouru serves all with the same commitment to quality, variety and fair pricing.
