Our Story

Built on Trust.
Driven by Value.

In 1991, the Mogaka family founded Uhuru Discount Centre on Hospital Road, Kisii — with one mission: bring genuine wholesale prices to the people of South Western Kenya.

Three decades on, that vision has become a regional powerhouse. Incorporated in 2004 as Ouru Superstores Limited, the business now spans 7 counties, employs 500+ people, and stocks 1,000+ products — with a sister company in real estate, Ouru Realtors Limited.
